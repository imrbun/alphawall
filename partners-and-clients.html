Partners / Clients

Partners

Cloudflare

Cloudflare is a global security and performance platform that protects and accelerates websites, applications, and networks. Its services include CDN, DDoS mitigation, Zero Trust security, and edge computing solutions, helping businesses enhance reliability, speed, and cyber-resilience.

Alibaba Cloud

Alibaba Cloud is a leading global cloud service provider offering scalable computing, storage, networking, and security solutions for businesses of all sizes. With extensive data center coverage across Asia and worldwide, it delivers reliable infrastructure, high-performance cloud products, and advanced AI capabilities tailored for digital transformation.

WorldFirst

WorldFirst is an international payment and global financial platform that enables businesses to manage cross-border payments, collections, and multi-currency accounts with ease. It supports global e-commerce operations, simplifies international settlements, and provides transparent, competitive exchange rates for companies expanding overseas.

Microsoft Azure

Microsoft Azure is a comprehensive cloud platform offering compute, data, AI, analytics, and security services for enterprise-grade applications. Its hybrid-cloud and multi-cloud capabilities allow organizations to integrate on-premise systems seamlessly, while maintaining strong governance, compliance, and global reliability across its worldwide data centers.

AxisNow

AxisNow is a cybersecurity technology provider specializing in network threat detection, intelligent inspection, and real-time incident response. Its solutions help enterprises identify unknown threats, secure internal networks, and strengthen overall IT security posture through smart analytics and automated monitoring.
